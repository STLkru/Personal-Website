Overview

  • Written in JAVA, Crazy Maze is a game in which a player attempts to make their way from the start point to the end point. The player must follow a series of narrow winding corridors with many false turns and dead-ends until they eventually reach the finish point. The game has many feautres that includes random map generation, difficulty level selection, and coin collection for high score achievements.
